What Is Return to Power?

Return to Power is an isometric sandbox MMORPG built for players who remember when MMOs were dangerous. A living hex-tile world where territory matters, skills are earned — not bought — and every season the world resets, giving everyone a fair shot at power.

🗺

Hex-Tile World

A procedurally generated world built on a hex grid. Claim tiles, build towns, and watch your territory grow — or fall.

⚔

Skill-Based Combat

No classes. No levels. Train what you use. Master swordsmanship, magic, stealth, or crafting — the choice is yours.

🔄

Seasonal Resets

The world resets each season. Your legend carries forward — your territory doesn't. Everyone starts the climb again.

🏘

Player Economies

Player-run shops, guild-owned markets, resource scarcity. The economy is entirely in players' hands.

✨

Modular Spell System

Build spells from components. Mix reagents, effects, and delivery types to create combinations no one has seen before. Every mage plays differently.

Built From the Ground Up

  • ✓ Isometric movement & UO-style targeting
  • ✓ 35-skill system with bucket caps (no skill dumping)
  • ✓ Modular spell system — mix components, reagents & effects
  • ✓ UO-style spell targeting flow (click to cast, RMB cancel)
  • ✓ Equipment with rarity tiers
  • ✓ Hex tile world editor with biome generation
  • ✓ Zone-based world with seamless travel
  • ⧗ Multiplayer networking (coming soon)
  • ⧗ Player housing & vendor system
  • ⧗ Guild & faction territory wars
  • ⧗ Seasonal world reset events
